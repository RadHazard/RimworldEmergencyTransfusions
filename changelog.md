# v1.0.0 - Initial Release
**Features**
- Command emergency blood transfusions via the right-click menu
  - Emergency transfusions always apply 1 hemogen pack.  They take the same time as a normal transfusion and restore the same amount of blood, but they can be done immediately without need of a medical bed
  - Doctors will use hemogen packs from their inventory, from the patient's inventory, from the ground, or from a caravan animal (in that order)
  - Emergency transfusions follow similar rules to draft tending:  The patient must be a member of your faction, a guest/prisoner/slave, or downed.
