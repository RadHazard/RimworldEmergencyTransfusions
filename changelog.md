# v1.0.0 - Initial Release
**Features**
- Command emergency blood transfusions via the right-click menu
  - There are two options:  Transfuse 1 pack, and transfuse as many as possible.
  - Emergency transfusions take the same time as a normal transfusion and restore the same amount of blood (35% per pack), but they can be done immediately without need of a medical bed, and even on pawns that are still conscious
  - Doctors will use hemogen packs from their patient's inventory, their own inventory, or the closest packs on the ground or in a caravan animal (in that order)
  - Emergency transfusions follow similar rules to draft tending:  The patient must be a member of your faction, a guest/prisoner/slave, or downed.  Animals can't be given transfusions (sorry!)
